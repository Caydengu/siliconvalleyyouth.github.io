WHAT WE DO

Classes

In order to broaden the intellectual and creative capabilities of middle to early high school students in the Silicon Valley community, we teach classes of advanced topics that students would normally not see until the last years of high school, if at all. In the past we have taught Economics, Debate, Current events, US History, AMC 8, Java, Website Design, Public Speaking, and more.

See All Classes

Community Service

Our leaders and students are currently volunteering in East Palo Alto to help students there. We donate all profits from classes to Ravenswood City School District. In 2017, we raised over $30,000, with which we sponsored an AVID writing competition for middle school students, purchased $5000 worth of gift cards to give to homeless students' families, and more.

Silicon Valley Youth is a student-run nonprofit educational and community service organization under the provisions of section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Founded in 2015 by Bryan and Benjamin Owens, then students of Gunn High School, SVY is now led by Brandon Fu and Sam Yang.

Silicon Valley Youth is an official certifying organization for the President's Volunteer Service Award.

All profits are donated to Ravenswood City School District to support kids in East Palo Alto.
