MathCounts

  • Description

This class is geared toward students who are about at the level of barely making the State Round of the Mathcounts exam. This class teaches challenging concepts which appear on the exam with a combination of lectures, practice problems, mock exams, and fun games such as mock Team and Countdown rounds. This class will cover geometry, algebra, counting/probability, and number theory. By the end of this class, students should be able to easily qualify for and excel at the State Round of Mathcounts.
