Basketball

  • Description
  • Class Photos

We will lead 5th graders to play basketball. We will teach them fundamental skills and do lots of drills. It will be fun!

Dates: 4/17, 4/24, 5/1, 5/8, 5/15, 5/22, 6/5

Time: 6:15pm-7:15pm

Location: Dilworth Elementary School, 1101 Strayer Drive, San Jose, CA 95129

Teacher

Max Zhang

Max is a 7th grader at Miller Middle School. He started playing basketball in 2nd grade, and has played basketball competitively for five years. He is a key player in NJB, Cupertino Hoops, AAU, Asian League, and Miller Basketball Team. He was selected into West San Jose NJB All Star Team in both 3rd and 4th grade. He was chosen as a member of West San Jose All Net Team in 5th and 6th grade. When Max played in the team as a 5th grader, All Net Team won bay area title and entered National NJB in LA representing the bay area. Max also plays card games and plays the drums. And he likes to listen to music and read books in his free time.

Teacher

Hi, I'm Leo Yang, and I am a 7th grader at Miller Middle School. I have played basketball for more than four years and have a lot of experience. I participate in after school debate, mathcounts, soccer and basketball. I also enjoy gaming and having fun.
