Science Bowl

  • Description
  • Class Photos
  • Testimonials

This is an introductory course to science bowl, a fast-paced jeopardy style buzzer competition. We will cover many different areas of science, such as Earth Science, Life Science, Chemistry, Biology, and Physics. This class is for students who are beginning or interested in science fields. We will have fun while learning about all the different areas of science in the process. Come Join Us!

Teacher

Oliver Ye

Oliver is a current Freshman at Saratoga High. He founded the Lawson science bowl club, and has been its president for two years. Oliver was a state qualifier for Math League, and has been on the AMC8 honor roll three times. In his free time, he enjoys cooking and woodworking. He has taught US History three times in the past, as well as Science Bowl once. He is very excited to work with his students!

Teaching Assistant

Tim Jing

Tim is an 8th grade student at Miller Middle School. He was Oliver's teaching assistant for the last two semesters. Tim is on the Miller Science Olympiad Team, placing second and fourth in the Science Olympiad California state competition. He has also been on the AMC 8 Honor Roll for two years. Tim is excited to work with his students!
