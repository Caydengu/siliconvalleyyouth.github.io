US History (Elementary School)

  • Description
  • Class Photos
  • Testimonials

In this class, students will learn about the United States history from the 1600s to now. Instead of covering just events, this class will also go over the fundamental concepts and ideas that sparked the events. In class, students will receive lectures and presentations about the weekly topic, while being occupied with documents and worksheets to examine. At the end of class, we will play a small game or watch an informative video for the students to better understand the subject. This format promotes learning while still having fun, so students can enjoy themselves as they go through the concepts of US History.

This class is meant for 5th and 6th graders.

Dates: 9/8, 9/22, 9/29, 10/6, 10/13, 10/27, 11/17, 12/1

Time: 7:00pm–8:30pm
