Business & Entrepreneurship

  • Description
  • Class Photos

Have you ever wanted to own your own business? Or startup a family bakery, tech company, or a tutoring service? Make an idea come to life? If you answer is yes to any of these questions, this course is right for you.

Students will be taught on the process of starting up their own business. During this course, students will be separated into multiple groups. With their groups, they will be able to start up their business, create a business plan and have the chance to pitch. Students will be educated on how to execute a startup. Students will also be led on the process of leading a successful company and where to go beyond that. Finally, this course also introduces students to several main subfields of business.

Good for 7th grade and above.

Dates: Saturdays 9/9, 9/16, 9/23, 9/30, 10/7, 10/14, 10/21, 11/4, 11/11, 11/18

Time: 4pm–5pm

Location: Gunn High School, 780 Arastradero Rd, Palo Alto, CA 94306
