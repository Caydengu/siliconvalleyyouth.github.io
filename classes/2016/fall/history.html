This class investigates the changing political atmosphere from the mid 1700s until today. In this class, you will read political documents from the past and discuss their impact on American history then and now.

This class is for 6th and 7th grade students.

Curriculum

  1. Colonization, Revolution, New Republic, Nationalism
  2. Westward Expansion, Civil War
  3. Industrialization, Gilded Age, Imperialism
  4. World War I, Great Depression, World War II
  5. Post-WWII, Cold War, Pax Americana, Era of Social Change
  6. The New Millennium (modern day) + jeopardy game + prizes
