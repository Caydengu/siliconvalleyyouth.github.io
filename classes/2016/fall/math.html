AMC 8/Mathcounts – Fall 2016

  • Description
  • Teaching Staff
  • Syllabus
  • Class Photos

Students will learn general concepts by using them to solve specific problems. Each lesson will have a handout with the problems to be lectured on, and there will be extra problems for practice. During the second half of class, students will work on the extra problems. Topics that will be covered include algebra, geometry, number theory, and combinatorics.

End result: Students will gain the necessary knowledge and skills for AMC 8 and Mathcounts.

Dates: Fridays 8/26, 9/09, 9/16, 9/23, 9/30, 10/07, 10/14, 10/21, 10/28, 11/04, 11/11, 11/18 (12 weeks; note that there is a break for Labor Day)

Time: 7:00–8:30 on Friday

Location: Santa Clara

This class is for middle school students.

Our Teaching Staff

Instructor: Andrew Zhao

Andrew Zhao is the most accomplished sophomore in math at Gunn High School. He is experienced in Mathcounts and AMC8 as he was part of the JLS Mathcounts team. When he was on the team, his team won 2nd in the Peninsula Chapter and 4th in Northern California. During high school, Andrew qualified for the AIME and his ARML team placed 5th in the Western Region. During his free time, he likes to work on challenging math and computer science problems.

Syllabus

  • Class 1: Introductions, Intro to Competition Math, Mock AMC8, Team round if time permits.
  • Class 2: Prime numbers, Factors, GCD, LCM
  • Class 3: Bases, Modular Arithmetic
  • Class 4: Variables, Linear Equations
  • Class 5: Ratios, Systems of Equations, Quadratics?
  • Class 6: Sequences + Series, Exponents
  • Class 7: Basic Counting, Venn Diagrams, Multiplication Principle, Casework
  • Class 8: Combinations + Permuations, Stars + Bars, Pascal’s triangle
  • Class 9: Probability, Expected Value, Binomial Probability
  • Class 10: Angles, Circles
  • Class 11: Area, Similarity, 3-D Geo
  • Class 12: Review + Mock Competition
