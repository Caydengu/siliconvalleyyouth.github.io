Current Events – Fall 2015

Dates: 8/24/2015 – 12/5/2015

Instructor: Bryan Owens

Location: Palo Alto

In this discussion-based class, students learned to use current events, often the cover story of TIME, as the starting point for thoughtful discussions about today's world. Students also read about six other articles from all different angles and opinions as supplemantary reading each week.

The class structure was round-table discussion. Bryan was the teacher and moderator. Every student participated in discussion and debate.
